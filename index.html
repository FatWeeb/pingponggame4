PING PONG GAME











Developed by Shawn Li

Instructions

  • First keep your laptop screens straight
  • Move yourself approximately 120 cm away from the laptop
  • Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on your wrist
  • Now press the 'Play' button, and again move 120 cm away from the laptop
  • The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
